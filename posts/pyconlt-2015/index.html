PyCon LT 2015 has been announced!

Annual Lithuanian Python conference PyConLT will be held on April 25th at VU MIF lecture hall 103, Naugarduko 24, Vilnius.

Conference starts at 11:00, language: Lithuanian.

Registration and more details (in Lithuanian): pycon.lt/2015.html

See you all there!
